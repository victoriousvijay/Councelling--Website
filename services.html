ALIGNMENT & PROGRESS

Career Counselling & Development

Feeling directionless or stuck in your corporate career is a clear sign that your professional anchors have diverged from your authentic personality strengths. My career counseling sessions help you map out a balanced, high-stability progression path.

Core Benefits of this service:

  • Strategic career change filters
  • Scientific strength profiling
  • Executive resume & portfolio confidence
  • Mid-career crisis navigation

Suitable Audience:

College graduates, mid-level executives undergoing burnout, individuals re-entering the workforce after career gaps, and professionals evaluating business pivots.

ACADEMIC PATHWAYS

Education & Stream Counselling

Selecting the wrong subject stream at age 15 or choosing an unaligned undergraduate major under social pressure is one of the leading causes of long-term career unhappiness. I provide unbiased, data-backed advisory to students and parents.

Core Benefits of this service:

  • Unbiased stream select parameters
  • College selection planning
  • Parent-teen expectation alignment
  • Study routines & stress coping

Suitable Audience:

High school students (Grade 9-12), college aspirants, undergraduates evaluating master's streams, and concerned parents.

EMOTIONAL WELL-BEING

Stress Management & Emotional Support

Life transitions, chronic academic pressure, or personal burnout can leave us feeling isolated, overwhelmed, and completely exhausted. I provide a highly secured, supportive listening space to rebuild coping structures.

Core Benefits of this service:

  • Mild anxiety & stress relief tools
  • Self-doubt & esteem recovery
  • Healthy emotional expression
  • Positive boundary habituation

Suitable Audience:

Individuals experiencing high stress, students facing performance anxieties, parents handling domestic burnout, and anyone needing a confidential, safe listening environment.

Relationship & Personal Support

Healthy, supportive relationships are built on strong boundaries and clear, constructive communication patterns. When repeating arguments or silence loops take over, neutral professional mediation helps restore balance.

Core Benefits of this service:

  • Conflict de-escalation skills
  • Constructive boundary setup
  • Neutral mediation loops
  • Enhanced mutual trust patterns

Couples facing communication difficulties, siblings seeking conflict resolution, family units navigating massive changes, and individuals working on boundary skills.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At the office of Mrs. Preeti Pareek, your privacy is our primary, absolute priority. This document outlines how we collect, use, and process any data you submit through our online booking engine and during physical or video counselling sessions.

1. Absolute Client-Counsellor Confidentiality

In accordance with the international standards of clinical psychology and professional development guidelines, all communications, assessments, discussion points, personal histories, and reports shared during counselling remain strictly confidential.

No details regarding your sessions, career choices, or emotional status will ever be disclosed to any third party (including parents, spouses, or educational institutions) without your explicit written authorization—except under specific ethical and legal exemptions described below:

  • A clear and immediate risk of severe self-harm or threat to the client's life.
  • A clear and immediate threat of harm to another identifiable person.
  • Specific legal directives, subpoenas, or official court summons where disclosure is legally required.

2. Information We Collect Online

When you book an appointment or submit a contact enquiry on this website, we collect the following basic details:

  • Identity Data: Full Name, Age Group, and Consent Status.
  • Contact Coordinates: Phone Number, Email Address.
  • Session Selections: Preferred service type (e.g., Career Counselling), preferred consultation mode, date, and hour.
  • Enquiry Details: A brief voluntary description of your concerns (we advise users never to submit emergency mental health details on this website).

3. Storage and local browser persistence

To demonstrate a fully functional booking system within this website, all booking submissions are stored directly inside the user's localized browser environment via localStorage. No remote server or unencrypted third-party datastore receives your form submissions from this client application. When we eventually integrate a secure production-grade API backend, your data will be transmitted using high-grade secure Transport Layer Security (TLS).

4. Accessing or Deleting your logs

You maintain full ownership of your data logs. Because details are saved on your local device, clearing your browser cache or application data will instantly and permanently erase all registered booking histories.

5. Contacting our Compliance Lead

If you have any questions, concerns, or queries regarding clinical privacy protocols, please reach out to us at privacy@[Counsellor Domain].com.

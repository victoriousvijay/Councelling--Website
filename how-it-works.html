THE STEPS TO CLARITY

How Your Counselling Works

A structured, completely confidential, and progressive roadmap designed to keep you feeling comfortable, in control, and safe at every stage of your personal or professional growth.

1
Step One

Select Your Counselling Service

Identify what immediate area you'd like to address. We offer distinct, scientifically grounded streams including: Career Assessment, Academic Stream Selection, Emotional Support, and Relationship/Personal Mediation.

Not sure where you fit? Simply select "Relationship & Personal Counselling" and we can discover your precise roadmap during our initial discussion call.

2
Step Two

Schedule Your Private Appointment

Pick a date, time slot, and communication mode that seamlessly integrates into your daily life. We offer robust flexibility:

  • Encrypted Video Consultation
  • Direct Phone Call
  • Safe In-Person Chamber Session
  • Easy Rescheduling Options

Once submitted, your lead is instantly synchronized with our Firestore dashboard. We will message you on WhatsApp to finalize details.

3
Step Three

Attend Your Private Counselling Session

This is a fully secure, non-judgmental, and comfortable environment. Together with Mrs. Preeti Pareek, you will break down immediate challenges, identify deep barriers, and map out your personality profile indicators using trusted, unbiased clinical frameworks.

Confidentiality Guarantee: We maintain strict psychological practice ethics. Your details and assessments will never be shared with outside parties without explicit written instruction.

4
Step Four

Receive Actionable Guidance & Support

Counselling doesn't end when the call terminates. You will receive a personalized, structured summary and strategic roadmap outlining concrete career directions, stream targets, or emotional coping tools.

We structure gentle check-ins to monitor your progress, evaluate milestones, and help you pivot your actions when external factors transition.

My Career Journey & Credentials

Over the last 12 years, my career has been dedicated to bridging psychological assessments with practical life resolutions. Having graduated as a University Gold Medalist with an MA in Psychology, I realized early on that abstract academic theories must translate into actionable, empathetic guidelines to genuinely support clients during severe career confusion, stream selection battles, and emotional distress.

Subsequently, I pursued a specialized Post Graduate Diploma in Guidance and Counselling. Since then, I have supported over 2,500+ clients—including school students, working executives undergoing massive career burnout, and couples navigating communication breakdowns.

Counselling Philosophy

My therapeutic models are deeply grounded in Person-Centered therapy and Cognitive Behavioral approaches. This means I do not see you as a 'case to be diagnosed'. Rather, I see you as an capable individual facing a temporary, complex intersection of choices.

I aim to facilitate self-discovery. By identifying your authentic personality parameters, value anchors, and capabilities through unbiased, empathetic dialogues, we co-create actionable roadmaps that lead directly to professional stability and mental clarity.

Core Ethical Guidelines Followed:

  • Strict Client-Counsellor Privacy
  • Unbiased Assessment Metrics
  • Non-judgmental Safe Listening
  • Compassionate, Client-Led Pace
